📊 AI Benchmark Hub

Leaderboards and evaluation platforms for comparing frontier AI models

Why so many? These benchmarks don't really disagree — they measure different things. Some focus on reasoning, others on coding, math, conversational quality, safety, or real-world usefulness. A model might top one leaderboard and lag on another. For a rounded view of any frontier model, cross-reference two or three benchmarks that match your use case.

Blind pairwise voting produces Elo ratings across chat, vision, and coding. The de facto "real-world quality" leaderboard
Free
WebDev Arena

Free
Dynabench
Dynamic, adversarial evaluation where humans generate challenge examples that fool current models
Free

A note on benchmark fatigue: many older benchmarks (SuperGLUE, GSM8K, original MMLU, AI2 ARC) are now saturated by frontier models — scores cluster near 100%, making them poor discriminators. The current frontier is measured by things like GPQA Diamond, SWE-bench Verified, Humanity's Last Exam, FrontierMath, and ARC-AGI. For a quick cross-provider snapshot, Artificial Analysis and LMArena are the most-consulted single pages.
